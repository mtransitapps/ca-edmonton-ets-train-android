This app adds Edmonton ETS Light Rail Transit information to MonTransit.

This app provides the schedule (offline and real-time) as well as news from @takeETSalert on Twitter.

ETS Light Rail Transit serve Edmonton in Alberta, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display Light Rail Transit information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by ETS (Edmonton Transit System).
http://www.edmonton.ca/transportation/ets/about_ets/ets-data-for-developers.aspx

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-edmonton-ets-train-android

This app is not related with ETS (Edmonton Transit System) and the City of Edmonton.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read real-time estimated next departures from etslive.edmonton.ca and to read news from Twitter
